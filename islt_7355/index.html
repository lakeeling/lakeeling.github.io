Welcome to my portfolio website! Thank you for taking the time to learn about me!

So, who am I? I am a high school library media specialist who has a passion for helping my students and coworkers find anything and everything they need to be successful. Whether it is helping a student select the perfect book, teaching a lesson on information literacy, offering technology help, or providing a quiet, safe space to relax, my goal is to make my library a place where everyone wants to be.

I designed this website to showcase my career and education. You’ll get to learn about me, what I have learned in my educational specialist degree program, and how I am applying that learning in my career. I’m excited to share a little bit about myself with you!
