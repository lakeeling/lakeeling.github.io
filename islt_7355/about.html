Lea Ann Keeling

I am a high school library media specialist in Carthage, Missouri. I love helping my students find reading and research materials. The library is my favorite place to be! I like to read and learn about lots of different topics.
I have a Master of Science in Human Development and Family Studies from the University of Arkansas. I also have a Master of Arts in Library Science from the University of Missouri. I am working on my Educational Specialist degree in Learning Technologies and Design with an emphasis in Technology in Schools. I am also working on an Online Educator Graduate Certificate. I plan to graduate from the University of Missouri in May 2022. In my free time, I like to read, watch the St. Louis Cardinals, and read while I watch the St. Louis Cardinals.
