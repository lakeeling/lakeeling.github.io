Reflection

My Degree Program

Ever since I earned my master’s degree in library science from Mizzou in 2011, I always had a feeling I would be back to get my educational specialist degree. As the years flew by, the timing was never right for me to return to school. When my school was shut down due to the pandemic in March 2020, I wanted to help teachers and students make the move to online learning. I felt that I lacked the skills to be a successful technology leader. That is when I decided to return to Mizzou for my EdS in Learning Technologies and Design with a Technology in Schools emphasis.


This EdS degree program was the perfect choice for me. I have gained both the knowledge and confidence I needed to become a technology leader in my school. The two classes that had the biggest impact on me were Technology Leadership and Teaching Online Courses. The Technology Leadership class put me into the role of a technology leader and taught me the importance of thinking through all steps of any technology implementation. Teaching Online Courses gave me the chance to design and teach an online course. I was nervous about presenting my lesson, but it ended up being so much fun! It was the highlight of my degree program.


Future Plans

I can’t believe how quickly these past two years at Mizzou have gone. I won’t be a graduate student much longer, but I will continue to learn how to use technology to design learning for my students. I use what I have learned in this degree program every day. My school went 1:1 with Chromebooks last year. I now have the skills to step up and help my students and teachers. Education has changed so much due to the pandemic, and I know that online learning and the use of technology tools will always be an important part of teaching and learning. One of the most important benefits of this program was being able to experience online learning and use technology tools as both a teacher and a student. I am so thankful to have had this experience and will use all I have learned to make a difference in my school.


While I am very happy in my current role, I am open to the idea of making a career change. I think I would enjoy being an academic librarian. If an opportunity comes up, I know that I will be ready to go for it. I am prepared for any career path I decide to take thanks to my job experience and this degree program. I look forward to whatever the future holds!
